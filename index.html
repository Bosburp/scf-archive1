ABOUT US

We’re a community dedicated to creating and sharing original, high-quality work, completely free and accessible to all.

On Lichess, many excellent studies can be difficult to find. Search results often bring unannotated or low-effort studies to the top, while high-quality, educational work remains buried. At the same time, the number of likes doesn’t always reflect true quality, allowing copied or less meaningful content to gain visibility.

Our community was created to address this. We aim to support high-quality creators and promote work that offers real value. Every study we share has been reviewed, with the goal of making high-quality content easier to find and appreciate.
