ABOUT US

We’re a community dedicated to creating and sharing original, high-quality work, completely free and accessible to all.

On Lichess, many excellent studies can be difficult to find. Search results often bring unannotated or low-effort studies to the top, while high-quality, educational work remains buried.

Our community was created to address this. We aim to support high-quality creators and promote work that offers real value. Every study we share has been reviewed, with the goal of making incredible content easier to find and appreciate.

SUBMIT A STUDY

We are always looking for high-quality, educational chess studies to add to our archive.

How to contribute:

All submissions are handled via our Discord server to ensure a proper peer-review process.

  1. Join our Discord Server.
  2. Navigate to the #study-submissions channel.
  3. Follow the pinned template to post your link safely.
