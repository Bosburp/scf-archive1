The SC&F Mission

The Chess Study Library was born from a simple necessity: a home for the finest interactive training materials created by the Study Creators & Friends community.

Managed by Bobby Punk, this archive serves as a digital lighthouse for chess enthusiasts, focusing on the preservation and organization of high-quality opening guides and tactical masterpieces.

Our philosophy is rooted in the "Classics"—valuing structured, elegant instruction over the noise of modern digital clutter.
